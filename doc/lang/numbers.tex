\section{Rounding}

These operations should work on any subtype of \df{real}.

\op{floor}{x}
\doc{Returns the largest integer less than or equal to {\em x}.}

\op{ceiling}{x}
\doc{Returns the smallest integer greater than or equal to {\em x}.}

\op{truncate}{x}
\doc{Could be defined {\tt (if (negative? x) (ceiling x) (floor x))}.}

\op{round}{x}
\doc{Returns nearest integer to {\em x}.  Ties are broken by rounding
to an even number.}
